Open Source Software Management Services

The role of open source software in developing new software is absolutely critical. We support OSS management through our deeply specialized subject matter expertise and industry knowledge of OSS compliance and security. Under the OSS management umbrella we provide OSS audit, OSS compliance and OSS security.  

Our team of highly qualified experts bring their knowledge of the OSS licensing and security to help develop an effective OSS policy.

OSS Audit

Knowledge is power. Know what is in your code. Unknown packages may expose your products to IP and security risks.

OSS License Compliance

OSS packages come in many flavors of licenses and require different compliance implementation.

OSS Security

OSS compliance require identification of OSS packages, vulnerabilities of OSS packages may become vulnerabilities of your product.
